Phase 3

Sheila Nazarian | Content Strategy

How the existing Netflix, IG, speaker-site, and press surfaces route to the scorecard and the captured-email backbone. No new content load on Dr. Nazarian.

The Premise

Dr. Nazarian already produces content at scale. Netflix (8 episodes), personal IG (1M, 5,651 posts), practice IG (225K), speaker-site press appearances (100+), public-advocacy appearances. The content stream is not the bottleneck. The routing from the content stream to a captured-email backbone is the bottleneck.

This strategy adds zero new content load. Every recommendation routes existing content through the scorecard and the captured-email backbone built in the Funnel Blueprint (Piece 03 and Piece 04).

Routing By Surface

Netflix series "Skin Decision: Before and After"

  • The series itself is the largest top-of-funnel asset Dr. Nazarian owns
  • Route: the Netflix-audience capture surface (Piece 05) lives on a dedicated domain or speaker-site subpath
  • The capture surface opens with a series-anchored framing in the speaker voice
  • Visitors enter the scorecard (Piece 03) at the top of the surface
  • Captured email tagged "source: netflix"
  • Quarterly Netflix-anniversary or series-recap content drop to the tagged segment as a re-engagement cadence (uses existing series IP, no new production)

@drsheilanazarian IG (1M followers)

  • Bio link routes to a link-in-bio aggregator pre-install
  • Post-install: bio link routes to the Netflix-audience capture surface OR a brand-router landing depending on the current campaign
  • Story-link routing: stories that mention a Spa26 service route directly to the Spa26 scorecard surface; stories that mention a Skin Spot product route directly to the Skin Spot scorecard surface; stories that mention a procedure route directly to the practice consultation surface with the VSL above it
  • Captured email tagged "source: drsheilanazarian-ig"
  • Zero new content. The story routing layers on top of the existing content stream.

@themodelsurgeon IG (225K followers)

  • Bio link routes to nazarianplasticsurgery.com pre-install
  • Post-install: bio link routes to the practice consultation surface with the VSL above the form (Piece 02), with the scorecard surfaced as a "Not sure yet?" alternative CTA on the same page
  • Captured email tagged "source: themodelsurgeon-ig"

Speaker site sheilanazarian.com

  • Currently lists the six brands as text references
  • Post-install: routed brand cards replace the text references. Each card opens to a one-paragraph context and routes through the scorecard for cold visitors or directly to the brand surface for returning visitors (cookie-tagged)
  • Captured email tagged "source: speaker-site"

Press appearances and podcast interviews (100+)

  • Existing press routes to Wikipedia or the speaker site
  • Post-install: every new press appearance gets a single tracked URL that routes to the Netflix-audience capture surface, tagged "source: press-{outlet}"
  • For evergreen press already published, a quarterly outreach round adds the capture-surface URL to the appearance's show notes where the host permits

Yelp profile (3,304 photos, 296 reviews)

  • Yelp does not route to AG1-built surfaces directly; the link in the Yelp profile points to the practice site
  • Post-install: practice-site VSL above the consultation form is the conversion lift; no Yelp content changes

What This Strategy Does Not Add

  • No new YouTube series
  • No new podcast
  • No new long-form video series
  • No new course content
  • No new public advocacy content
  • No additional posting cadence on any IG account

The strategy is routing infrastructure for the content stream Dr. Nazarian already runs. Every install layer compounds against the existing content cadence.

Voice Note On Tone Across The Content Layer

The newsletter, the welcome flow, the scorecard readouts, the VSL, and the capture-surface copy all stay inside the regulated-medical register. The educational frame is the default; the journey-stage routing is the layer above; the brand-of-origin tagging is the data layer underneath. No surface anywhere across the AG1-built content layer makes a procedure-efficacy claim, a cosmetic-outcome promise, or a "you will look X" framing.

The Netflix audience knows Dr. Nazarian's voice from the series. The IG audience knows the voice from the post stream. The speaker-site audience knows the voice from press appearances. The AG1-built layer carries that voice into the captured-email backbone and the cross-brand routing without inventing a new tone for the surface.
