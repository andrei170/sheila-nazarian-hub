The Observable Gap

Six brands. 2M+ aggregate social audience. Zero cross-brand handoff infrastructure on the public surface. The audience on the Netflix-driven IG (1M) and on the Plastic Surgery practice IG (225K) and on the speaker site (1.78M aggregate) does not route through a readiness scorecard, does not see a VSL above any consultation or any product checkout, does not get tagged by buyer type into a segmented welcome flow after a Spa26 booking or a Skin Spot purchase, and does not get routed across brands (Plastic Surgery to Spa26 to Skin Spot, or Spa26 to Plastic Surgery, or Skin Spot to Spa26).

The brands work. The bridges between them do not exist yet.
