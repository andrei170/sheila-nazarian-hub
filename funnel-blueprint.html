Each piece installs in under two weeks per piece from kickoff. Sequenced this way so the scorecard and the captured-email backbone feed every downstream piece from day one.

What This Plan Does Not Change

  • Procedure menu, service catalog, product catalog
  • Pricing on individual procedures (we never quote procedure prices in any AG1-built surface)
  • Clinical protocols, regulatory framing, compliance posture
  • Brand aesthetics, logo, primary palette (unless explicitly scoped on day one)
  • The Netflix series IP or licensing
  • The Nazarian Institute non-profit mission
  • The practice's stated hero positioning ("Beauty With Integrity Transparency Honesty Authenticity")

What Sits Underneath Every Piece

A regulated-medical register. Every AG1-built surface stays at the level of educational content, journey-stage guidance, and patient-fit framing. No income claims. No cosmetic-result promises. No "you will look X" framing. No procedure efficacy claims. The scorecard surfaces readiness, not a clinical clearance. The welcome flow routes by brand-of-origin, not by predicted outcome. The cross-brand handoff is a navigation system, not a treatment plan.

The footer disclaimer carries across every AG1-built surface for the practice and Spa26 builds: "Educational content only. Not regulated medical advice. Cosmetic procedures carry inherent risks; outcomes vary by patient and procedure. Consult a board-certified medical professional in your jurisdiction."
